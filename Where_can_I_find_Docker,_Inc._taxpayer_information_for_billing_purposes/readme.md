## Questions

* Our company is using Docker services, but in order to treat them as a company expense we are required to obtain a Certificate of Tax Residence.
* Where can I find the billing information for Docker, Inc.? The most important information needed is the RUT/NIF/TIN/EIN/ID of the company.

## Answer

The Docker, Inc. taxpayer information is available via the attached file **[docker-inc-taxpayer-id.pdf](https://success.docker.com/@api/deki/files/313/docker-inc-taxpayer-id.pdf?revision=1 "docker-inc-taxpayer-id.pdf")**.

[2016 Docker Form 6166.pdf](https://success.docker.com/@api/deki/files/314/2016_Docker_Form_6166.pdf?revision=1 "2016 Docker Form 6166.pdf") is also available for download. The watermark is visible due to the scan process.
