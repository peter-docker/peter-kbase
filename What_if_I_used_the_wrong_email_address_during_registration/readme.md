If you mistyped your email address or used the wrong email during Docker Hub or Docker Cloud registration, open a [Customer Service Request](https://success.docker.com/Support "Account Support Form") and include your username and the mistyped email address.

If you have access to your account and need to change your email address, please view the [How do I change my email address?](https://success.docker.com/Support/How_do_I_change_my_email_address%3F?mt-draft=true "How do I change my email address?") article.
