If you would like to change your email address, follow the steps below.

## Steps on Docker Cloud

1. Log into [Docker Cloud](https://cloud.docker.com/ "https://cloud.docker.com/") on the browser.
2. Click on your account name at the far right of the header, then select your username that you wish to update.
3. Click again on your username, then select **Account Settings**.
4. In the **User **Tab, change the primary email address or add additional ones.

## Steps on Docker Hub

1. Log in to [Docker Hub](https://hub.docker.com/ "https://hub.docker.com/") on the browser.
2. Click on your account name at the far right of the header.
3. By default you are in the **Account Settings** tab, change the primary email address or add additional ones.

If you have used the incorrect email during registration please visit the [What if I used the wrong email address during registration?](https://success.docker.com/Support/What_if_I_used_the_wrong_email_address_during_registration%3F "https://success.docker.com/Support/What_if_I_used_the_wrong_email_address_during_registration%3F") article.
