If you are getting double billed for your Docker Hub subscription, you may have a subscription for both your personal account and your organization account.

* Check your personal subscription at <https://hub.docker.com/account/billing-plans/>
* Check your organization subscription by clicking **Organizations** in the top navigation bar and then clicking **Billing**.
