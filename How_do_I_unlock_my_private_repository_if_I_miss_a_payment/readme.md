If you have a delinquent account with private repositories, your private repositories will be locked until your bill is paid in full.

Once all late payments have been made, the repository will be unlocked within minutes. If a repository is not unlocked after payment is made, please contact [Support](https://support.docker.com/ "https://support.docker.com").
