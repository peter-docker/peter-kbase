You can restart your Docker Trusted Registry ( assuming its`DTR``2.0.x`) from your Universal Control Plane (UCP) UI.

Go to your UCP UI, and click on **Applications** in the **RESOURCES** menu on the left side. Check **Docker Trusted Registry 2.0.x - (Replica xxxx)**, and then select **Restart**, as shown (make sure that **all** containers are selected):
