To add or remove an [Authorized Contact](/article/What_is_the_difference_between_Support_Administrators_and_Authorized_Contacts%3F "Docker Datacenter - Support Administrators and Authorized Contacts"), the account [Support Administrator](/article/What_is_the_difference_between_Support_Administrators_and_Authorized_Contacts%3F "Docker Datacenter - Support Administrators and Authorized Contacts") must contact [Docker Support](https://success.docker.com/Support "Get Help"). Please provide the following with your request:

* Full name
* Email address
* Docker/Hub ID
* Telephone Number (with country code)
