If you need to unlink your GitHub account, please follow the steps below. 
1. Log into [Docker Hub](https://hub.docker.com) on the browser.
1. Click on your account name at the far right of the header.
1. By default you are in the **Account Settings** tab.
1. Click **Linked Accounts & Services**.
1. Click **Unlink GitHub**.
